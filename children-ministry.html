Children Ministry 兒童事工

Children are valued because they are children of God, central to God's covenant with His people. At NYCBC, we believe that Children Ministry is important not just for the future of our church, but for the Kingdom of God. We see children as who they are: as growing spiritual beings with full personhood and absolute individuality. With the help of parents, volunteers, and pastoral staff, our ministry team strives to support parents to further the faith formation and character development of our children. Apart from regular Sunday ministry, we offer various multi-format events to support our children's life journey. All this aims to offer a relevant context for children to understand the work of God in their lives.

兒童被看為寶貴，是因為他們是上帝的兒女，是上帝與祂的子民立約的核心。在 NYCBC，我們相信兒童事工不僅關乎教會的未來，對神的國度同樣至關重要。我們認為兒童是靈命成長、擁有完整人格和獨立個性的群體。在父母、義工和牧師團隊的支持下，我們的事工團隊致力於幫助父母促進孩子的信心建立與性格發展。除了常規的主日事工，我們還提供各種形式的活動，支持兒童在人生旅程中的成長。所有這些事工旨在為兒童創造有意義的環境，幫助他們明白上帝在他們生命中的工作。

Children Sunday School 兒童主日學

Join Us for Children's Sunday School!

We are excited to invite your children to our Sunday School, held every Sunday morning. Our classes are open for Pre-School through Grade 6, offering a joyful and nurturing environment for your little ones to learn and grow in their faith.

If you're new here, please take a moment to fill out our "New Student Registration Form". Feel free to reach out if you have any questions, we can't wait to welcome you and your children into our community. See you on Sunday!

兒童主日學歡迎您！

誠意邀請您的孩子參加每週日舉行的兒童主日學。專為學前班至六年級的小朋友而設，兒童主日學提供一個充滿喜樂與愛心的環境，讓孩童在信仰中學習與成長。
