# Children Ministry / 兒童事工
**URL:** /children-ministry

## Scripture
"Let the little children come to me and do not hinder them, for to such belongs the kingdom of heaven." Matthew 19:14
「讓小孩子到我這裏來，不要禁止他們；因為在天國的，正是這樣的人。」馬太福音19:14

## Philosophy
Children are valued because they are children of God, central to God's covenant with His people. Children ministry matters for both the church's future and God's kingdom. Children are growing spiritual beings with full personhood and absolute individuality. The ministry aims to support parents in faith formation and character development.
