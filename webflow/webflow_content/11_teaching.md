## Course Descriptions

### Joshua Study — "Moving Into a New Generation with the Eternal Covenant God"
Explores Leadership and Land themes, showing how God led Israel into Canaan despite challenges, emphasizing grace, faithfulness, courage, and obedience.

### Acts Selection — "10 People, 10 Events That Overturned an Era"
Examines ten key figures and pivotal events, demonstrating how ordinary people became vessels overturning history under the Holy Spirit's guidance.

### Youth Class — "A Life in Flight"
Multi-modal teaching designs to make Scripture engaging and interactive, systematically helping students understand Jesus's life and teachings.

### Young Believers — "Complete Guide to Work"
Work is not a curse but a mission; not merely livelihood but participation in God's kingdom.

### Matthew Gospel — "Entering Matthew: Seeing and Following Jesus"
A journey to deeply know Jesus, suitable for seekers, new Christians, and those wanting biblical grounding.

### Seeker Class — "The Gospel That Changes Lives"
A series of compelling biblical passages introducing the gospel that transforms life through Jesus's incarnation.

### Gospel Class
Foundational gospel teachings progressively for those beginning to engage with church and Scripture.
