# Giving / 奉獻
**URL:** /giving

## Scripture
因為我們得救是本乎恩、也因著信，這是神所賜的免費禮物。以弗所書2:8

## Introduction
Giving acknowledges everything belongs to God and represents worship participation in gospel ministry. Members can place an "offering card" in the collection bag as a symbolic gesture when using online giving methods.

## Giving Methods

### Option 1: Online Giving / 網上奉獻
- Pre-authorized Debit (monthly or one-time)
- Interac e-Transfer to: **e-offering@nycbc.ca**

### Option 2: Cheques / 支票奉獻
- Write payable to "NYCBC"
- Mail or deliver to office

### Option 3: Cash / 現金奉獻
- Deliver during office hours
- Do NOT mail cash

### Option 4: Securities Donations / 投資項目奉獻
- Download PDF forms for investment/securities giving
- Potential tax benefits on capital gains
