# Servant Development Ministry (SDM) / 僕人建立事工
**URL:** /sdm

## Programs

### FaithlifeTV
The church has subscribed to video resources from faithlife.com, offering hundreds of Logos mobile education course videos. Members can register to access.

### Discipleship Training Groups / 門徒訓練小組
For members who want to learn to follow the Lord more seriously and pursue growth in God's will. North York Cantonese Congregation Discipleship Training Small Groups offer growth through peer mentoring relationships.
