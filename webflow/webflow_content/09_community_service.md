# Community Service / 社群服務
**URL:** /community-service

## Tagline
愛心，開心···展關懷 (Love, Joy, and Care)

## Description
A group of people work diligently every week in the gymnasium, classrooms, and kitchen, providing fitness, learning, and fellowship programs.
