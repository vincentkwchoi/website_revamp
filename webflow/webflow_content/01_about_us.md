## Welcome Message
歡迎!

NYCBC is a community seeking to know God and glorify Him through Jesus Christ. We aim to serve visitors by sharing Jesus — the foundation and reason for our existence.

Members, like the pastor, are willing to care for visitors and their families, helping with spiritual growth. The church welcomes those exploring faith or deepening their relationship with Christ through worship and study of Scripture grounded in practical living.

Jesus taught that disciples are identified through obedience to His words and mutual love. NYCBC members commit to practicing Christ's teachings in daily life.

We sincerely invite you to learn and grow together in Christ. Please continue browsing our website. If you have any questions, we are happy to contact you.
