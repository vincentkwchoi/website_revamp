# Our Faith / 我們的信仰
**URL:** /our-faith

## Mission Statement / 使命宣言
堅立神子民，恩福及萬人
(Establish God's people, bless all humanity)

## Vision Statement / 異象宣言
The congregation commits to:
- Christ-centered worship
- Bible-based teaching
- Community relationships
- Service ministry
- Community outreach
- Lifestyle evangelism
- Strategic church planting
- Global missions

Through the Holy Spirit's power, they seek to fulfill the greatest commandment and the Great Commission from their local community throughout the Greater Toronto Area to the ends of the earth.

## Doctrinal Statements / 我們的信仰

### 聖經 (Scripture)
全部新舊約聖經是神完整的話語；是聖靈所默示，信仰與行為的唯一準則

### 神 (God)
Belief in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit as the triune God

### 耶穌基督 (Jesus Christ)
Divine-human nature, incarnation, crucifixion for redemption, resurrection, ascension

### 聖靈 (Holy Spirit)
Full deity and personhood; convicts of sin, grants rebirth, sanctification

### 人 (Humanity)
Created in God's image; fallen through sin; spiritually dead without redemption

### 救恩 (Salvation)
Grace-based redemption through Christ's substitutionary death and resurrection

### 末後的事 (Eschatology)
Christ's bodily return; resurrection of righteous and unrighteous

### 地方教會 (Local Church)
Composed of believers, self-governing under Christ's headship

### 聖禮 (Ordinances)
Baptism (full immersion) and Communion (bread and cup commemoration)
