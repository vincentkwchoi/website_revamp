# Our History / 我們的歷史
**URL:** /our-history

## Founding
In the early 1970s, Toronto had few Chinese churches, concentrated near Chinatown. Concerned believers sought to establish a congregation in residential areas distant from the downtown core.

在一九七零年六月初假借位於 Victoria Park 及 Lawrence 一間教會開始聚會，稱為「以連華人浸信會」
(In early June 1970, they began meeting at a church located at Victoria Park and Lawrence, calling themselves Elim Chinese Baptist Church)

## Key Milestones
| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1970 | Founded as Elim Chinese Baptist Church |
| 1975 | Purchased current property at 675 Sheppard Ave. E. |
| 1977 | Main sanctuary constructed |
| 1978 | Renamed to 北約華人浸信會 (North York Chinese Baptist Church) |
| 1982 | Educational building added |
| 2001 | New wing constructed |
| 2005 | Purchased adjacent property at 685 Sheppard Ave. E. |

## Pastoral Leadership
Led successively by pastors Feng Jin, Chan Hei-him, Lee Fei-ng, and Zeng Yong-guang.
Emphasis on: 敬拜、禱告、讀經、團契及傳福音 (worship, prayer, scripture reading, fellowship, and evangelism)
